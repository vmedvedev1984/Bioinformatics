Джукса-Кантора или обобщенную обратимую во времени (GTR)
аминокислоты:
JTT ( Jones-Taylor-Thornton, 1992 ), WAG ( Whelan & Goldman, 2001 ), LG ( Le and Gascuel, 2008 )

Protein alignment with the JTT+CAT model:
FastTree < alignment_file > tree_file or FastTree alignment.file > tree_file

Nucleotide alignment with the GTR+CAT model:
FastTree -gtr -nt < alignment.file > tree_file or FastTree -gtr -nt alignment_file > tree_file
